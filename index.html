Test type

Speed is what you type into Orca; flow (speed × layer-height × line-width) is what actually prints and is comparable across printers. LH + line width only convert between them.

Adaptive PA depends on both flow and acceleration. Leave accel values blank to auto-scale 5 steps from 1000 to your max (Orca suggests 1000, 2000, 4000, 8000, 12000, 16000). The test grid is every flow × every accel.
